FAQ

Common Questions

Is this program eligible for employer reimbursement?
Yes. The program includes a formal fee breakdown document (itemized retail value), a certificate of completion documenting 12 CPD hours, and a document reference number — all designed to support corporate expense and professional development reimbursement submissions.
Do I need a technical background in AI?
No. This program is specifically designed for IT delivery professionals — not engineers or data scientists. No coding experience is required. The curriculum focuses on governance, readiness, and leadership — not technical implementation.
How much time does each session require?
Each session is 90 minutes of live virtual instruction over Zoom or Microsoft Teams. The program runs across 8 weeks with 1 session per week. Between-session work is applied directly to your current role — not additional homework.
What happens if I miss a session?
Sessions are recorded. Participants who miss a live session receive access to the recording and can submit questions asynchronously. All deliverables are still produced and reviewed.
When does the next cohort begin?
Cohort dates are released on a rolling basis with limited enrollment per intake. Contact [email protected] to be added to the priority notification list.
Who is this program presented by?
This program is presented by AI Ready Leaders — a professional advancement institute for AI-powered leaders, and an initiative of Arrell Advisory, AI Governance and Transformation Consulting.
